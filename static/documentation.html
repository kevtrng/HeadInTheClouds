HEAD IN THE CLOUDS

Head in the Clouds is a web application that allows you to post your thoughts! Share your thoughts with other users by creating a new Cloud. You can also read other people's Clouds on our Cloud Wall!

Features

Mobile Friendly

All pages are designed using a mobile-first approach

JavaScript (JQuery)

Our Cloud Wall includes a scroll to top button. Useful when you get lost in everyone's thoughts.

Creating a Cloud

To create a cloud enter your email, thought, and mood. Please ensure that you:
  • do not leave any fields blank
  • enter an email with a valid format
  • Enter a thought that is 1-50 characters long
If any of the above conditions are not met, your Cloud will not make it to the Cloud Wall!

Viewing Clouds

The Cloud Wall allows you to view yours and other users' Clouds.

Since we have an "endless scroll" format, our scroll to top button will ensure you don't have to scroll for miles to get back to the top of the page
